Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day setups?

Yes — depending on availability and location. Contact us on WhatsApp for instant confirmation. We always try to accommodate urgent requests when possible.

Can we customize themes?

Absolutely — we create custom themes as per your request. Share your ideas, color preferences, or Pinterest inspirations and we'll bring them to life!

How do I pay?

We accept UPI, cash on delivery, or online payment on confirmation. A 50% advance is required to confirm your booking, with the balance payable on the event day.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Bangalore and surrounding areas. Additional charges may apply for locations beyond 30km from the city center.
